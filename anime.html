Watching anime & reading manga

Even before I saw my first anime, I was reading books about world history and different cultures.
I think I was about 7 years at that point. And already then I was attracted to the Asian and Japanese culture particularly.


I think the first anime that I've ever seen was Pokemon when I was about 9 years old.
I didn't even know what anime is at that point. I just like a cartoon with cute creatures.


Few years later I saw Naruto and Shaman King on the TV and I felt in love with it.

A couple of years later I only found that anime is based on manga. And I found out reading manga can be even more interesting than just watching anime.

My favorite title at this moment is "Golden Kamuy".
I like the setting as it's connected to the world history. It is Japan after the Russo-Japanese war.
In short, it is about a man, Saichi Sugimoto, that is looking for a treasure to heal and provide his friend's widow of his dead comrade.
